## Overview

Equannabis is one of Ecuador's first companies to build a legal, sustainable cannabis and hemp operation — and they needed a film that told that story honestly.

As director, cinematographer, and editor, I spent time with the team across Ecuador's coastal and highland regions, documenting the early days of the company: the first harvests, the science behind their process, and the people driving it forward.

The result is a short documentary that treats its subject with the same seriousness the company brings to its work.

---

## Role

Full production — development, shooting, and post-production handled independently.

- **Direction & cinematography:** handheld, natural light, observational style
- **Editing:** paced to reflect the rhythm of agricultural work, not a corporate pitch
- **Sound:** location audio + original score
