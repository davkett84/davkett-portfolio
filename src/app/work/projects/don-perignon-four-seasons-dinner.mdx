## Overview

An exclusive private dinner hosted at **Mina’s Fish House**, one of Oahu’s most renowned restaurants, located within **Four Seasons Resort Ko Olina**.  
The evening was curated around the launch and tasting of a new **Don Perignon** release, blending fine dining, design, and atmosphere into a refined, intimate experience.

The series captures the essence of the night through subtle moments — champagne poured under warm light, curated table details, and guests fully immersed in the rhythm of the evening.
