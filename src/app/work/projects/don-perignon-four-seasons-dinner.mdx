## Overview

Some events aren't meant to be documented — they're meant to be felt. This was one of them.

An exclusive private dinner at **Mina's Fish House**, inside **Four Seasons Resort Ko Olina**, built around the launch of a new **Dom Pérignon** release. The guest list was curated. The setting was precise. The only brief was to capture it without interrupting it.

The resulting series focuses on atmosphere over spectacle — champagne catching warm light, table details composed with intention, and the quiet energy of people who know how to be present in a room like that.
