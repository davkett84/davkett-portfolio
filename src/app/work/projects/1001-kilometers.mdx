## Overview

1001 Kilometers is a visual documentation of endurance, precision, and momentum.

Commissioned by the winning team, this series captures the full narrative arc of race day — preparation in the paddock, mechanical intensity on track, strategic pauses in the pit lane, and the defining moment of victory at the finish line.

Rather than focusing solely on speed, the images explore atmosphere, tension, and the human presence behind performance. Each frame reflects the rhythm of competition — lap after lap — until the final kilometer sealed the result.
