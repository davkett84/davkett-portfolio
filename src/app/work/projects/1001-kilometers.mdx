## Overview

Endurance racing is not about a single moment — it's about everything that accumulates before one.

Commissioned by the winning team, this series documents the full arc of race day at the 1001KM: the pre-dawn paddock setup, mechanical precision on track, calculated decisions in the pit lane, and the moment the checkered flag confirmed what the team had fought hours to achieve.

The work focuses less on speed and more on presence — the athletes, the crew, the tension between laps. Competition at this level is as much psychological as it is mechanical, and these images try to reflect that.
