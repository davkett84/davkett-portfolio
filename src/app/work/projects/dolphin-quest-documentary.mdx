## Overview

**The Dolphin Quest Documentary** is a short film produced in collaboration with *The Kahala Hotel & Resort* — one of Hawaii's most iconic luxury properties.

The film goes beyond the surface of the program to explore what actually happens between trainers and dolphins every day: the patience, the trust, and the quiet language that develops between two very different species.

---

## Approach

Shot entirely on-site at the Dolphin Lagoon at The Kahala, the film combines intimate trainer interviews, environmental soundscapes, and natural light cinematography to create something that feels more like a portrait than a promotional piece.

The goal was never to make a marketing video. It was to make something honest.
